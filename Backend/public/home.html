Our Services

We provide a variety of services to meet your needs.

Incident Reporting System

Develop a feature that allows users to report incidents or crimes, providing details such as location, time, type of crime, and any additional information.

Suspect Tracking and Management

Create a module to track and manage information related to suspects, including identification details, known associates, criminal history, and current status in the investigation.

Mobile App Integration

Build a mobile application that allows users to report incidents, receive updates on case status, access victim support services, and receive alerts and notifications from law enforcement agencies.

Emergency Response Integration
